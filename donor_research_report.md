# Troy VEX Robotics — Donor & Sponsor Research Report
**Program:** Troy High School NJROTC VEX Robotics | Fullerton, CA
**Report Date:** March 2026
**Prepared For:** Troy VEX Leadership Team

---

## Executive Summary

This report compiles **70+ verified donor and sponsor prospects** across four geographic tiers. Troy VEX is in an excellent position to attract funding because it sits at the intersection of three high-priority giving categories for most of these funders: **STEM education**, **military/JROTC youth programs**, and **underserved/underrepresented community impact**.

### Key Highlights

- **JROTC is a significant fundraising asset.** Multiple national sponsors (RECF, US Army, DoD STEM) specifically fund JROTC robotics teams. The RECF has a dedicated JROTC partnership and cadets may access special funding. Contact: jrotc@recf.org immediately.
- **The Beckman Foundation** (Irvine, CA) funds OC youth robotics explicitly — rolling applications, no 501c3 required if partnered with one.
- **RTX/Raytheon has a major facility in Fullerton.** This creates a direct local tie for community grants; RTX grants through employees (requires RTX employee champion).
- **Northrop Grumman Foundation** is a major RECF partner and gives specifically to Anaheim/SoCal schools (Scaling Up for STEM Initiative, 250-mile radius from Anaheim).
- **The Fullerton Education Foundation** offers Innovation Seed Grants and P.I.E. Grants directly to Fullerton School District teachers and schools.
- **Google.org** just committed $10 million to RECF for middle school robotics — worth direct outreach.
- **Eaton Corporation** is an active RECF World Championship sponsor offering team and event grants.
- **Chevron** has a longstanding partnership with RECF specifically supporting California State Championships.

### Priority Action Flags Used
- **[HIGH PRIORITY]** = Specifically mentions JROTC, military programs, or has direct local OC/Fullerton tie
- **[APPLY NOW]** = Rolling/open application as of March 2026
- **[501c3 REQUIRED]** = Must apply through a 501c3 organization
- **[EMPLOYEE CHAMPION]** = Requires an employee of that company to sponsor/submit the application
